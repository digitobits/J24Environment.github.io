OUR TEAM

James Mulolo

Director/Principal

James Mulolo has an advanced University Degree and over 18 years relevant international strategic leadership and supervision experience in the international development arena.

James is a professional trainer with knowledge and the understanding of business management theories, concepts and approaches of his work. He communicates clearly and effectively by listening to others, correctly interprets messages from others and responds appropriately. He can identify key issues in a complex situation and comes to the heart of the problem quickly though he always considers the positive and negative impacts of decisions prior to making them.

As a team leader, James has a passion for this business and has brought that into the firm by ensuring every team member is passionate about providing quality goods and services to J24 Environment Resource’s clients for them to do more in their business.

Since inception, J24 Environment Resource’s has built a solid business foundation, all while delivering sustainable growth in the midst of a challenging operating environment. It will continue to face many challenges in its business environment. However, it is quite adept at adopting change, and this has allowed it to continue on a growth trajectory. Its aim is to align its actions to meet client needs. To achieve this, J24 Environment Resource’s main strategic focus is on continuous analysis of market trends and organisational transformation. It is confident that it will exceed expectations of all its stakeholders by carefully considering their interests.
